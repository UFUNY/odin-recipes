Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna layers tender noodles with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. Perfect for feeding a crowd or saving leftovers for the week.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheese
  3. From there, its just an assembly job
  4. Bake until youre ready to eat
