Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta that is a staple in Italian cuisine and popular worldwide.
It's typically made from durum wheat semolina and water, and is versatile, pairing well with a wide variety of sauces like tomato, pesto, or carbonara.

Ingredients

  • Seminola flour
  • Water
  • Grounf Meat
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Get your supplies
  2. Cooking the meat + get the water to boil
  3. Mixing the sauce
  4. Cooking the spaghetti
  5. Serving your spaghetti
